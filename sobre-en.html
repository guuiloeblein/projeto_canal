A little about myself

Since I was very young I was interested in computers, I got my first one at 11 years old and I was fascinated, the machine itself wasn't the best, it didn't run any games and the dial-up internet at the time didn't help either, I needed to change that.
It was then that I met my first love of IT “Computer architecture and maintenance” I started to disassemble and assemble the entire machine, and combined with the system optimization that I learned together, I finally managed to run the games of the time.
I grew up knowing my passion, but I was always a little self-taught, and that's why it took me a long time to get into college, I decided to do computer engineering, I studied about 3 years and then I met my second love of IT and that made me change the direction of my studies, “Programming” I currently changed my course and I am in the last semester of Systems Analysis and Development at Cruzeiro do Sul College.
Programming is the best tool that a human being can have, a programmer has the power to build a web page, but also software that can change the world, is there anything more fascinating than that?
